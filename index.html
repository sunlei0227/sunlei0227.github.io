Currently I am a Postdoc researcher at Tsinghua University, cooperate with Prof. Qiangfeng Cliff Zhang. I will start my own lab in Shandong University (Qingdao). in November of 2022. I am broadly interested in RNA biology, espcially RNA structrual biology, RNA vaccine and adaptor design, anti- RNA virus drugs development (such as SARS-CoV-2). My lab is to develop and use a combination of computational and experimental methods to interpret the structural and functional relationships of biological macromolecules, build the interaction network, uncover the regulation mechanism and find the potencail therapy for diseases by high-throughput sequencing, bioinformatics analysis, artificial intelligence (AI) prediction, and experimental validation.

Prior to Postdoc period, I received my Ph.D. degree in the School of Life Science, Tsinghua University in 2019. Before I came to Tsinghua University, I received a bacholar degree in Taishan College, Shandong University in 2014.
